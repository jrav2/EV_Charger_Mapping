Understanding Charger Types

Level 1

Standard household outlet (120V). Best for overnight charging at home.

2-5 miles of range per hour

Level 2

240V charging, common at public stations and home installations. Ideal for daily charging.

10-60 miles of range per hour

DC Fast Charging

High-powered rapid charging for long trips. Can charge most EVs to 80% in 20-40 minutes.

180-250+ miles of range per hour
